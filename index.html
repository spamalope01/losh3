Structural Design and Planning

Residential

Whether you're looking to remodel your home, build a new custom home, or design a new multi-building apatment complex, L2 Engineers can help. We will work with you and your team to produce efficient and robust structural designs.

Site Structures

L2 Engineers has significant experience in structural design associated with site development, including multiple retaining wall types (gravity and deeply embedded), and reinforced concrete detention and infiltration vaults.

Commercial

L2 Engineers has experience in commercial design projects including both new structure and tenant improvement.

Projects

Here are some examples of our most recent projects.

Norkirk DevelopmentThis project was a four lot development in the Norkirk neighborhood in Kirkland, WA. These houses are two story craftsman with a partial basement. Due to limited available wall width, high capacity premanufactured wood shear wall systems were specified.

Williams ResidenceWe worked closely with the architect to design this custom high end house with significant portions of exposed structure. Due to adjacency to water and local topography, the design wind forces on this house are exceptionally high. High associated uplift and lateral forces effects were resolved using customized structural assemblies.

Kent CommercialThis structure is a 10,500 square foot wood framed retail building with 7 units. The roof consists of variable depth trusses supported on two beam lines. The walls are conventionally framed wood shear walls.

Gamroth ResidenceThis residential remodel project consisting of multiple additions to the building footprint and revisions to the floor layouts, located on Mercer Island, WA. Lateral retrofit of this structure was complicated by older construction methods used in the original construction of this house.

Who We Are

L2 Engineers is a family run Structural Engineering Firm. We specialize in all aspects of residential design, ranging from small remodels and retrofits, to the design of new structures and associated site structure (retaining walls, detention vaults etc.) We also have significant experience with a variety of commercial structures. As our business model relies on reducing overhead costs, we are therefore able to offer highly responsive service at competitive rates.
